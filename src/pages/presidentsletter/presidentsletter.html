Presidents Letter

Thank you for joining us at the 60th anniversary of the Atlanta Marketer of the Year Awards (AMYs) at the College Football Hall of Fame.

For 60 years, the AMY Awards program has put a spotlight on the most creative, innovative and successful work done by Atlanta’s Marketing Community. The quality and creativity of this year’s entries clearly showcase the incredible talent within our community.

The 2017 AMY Awards Program will recognize and honor the work of local companies and agencies in over 20 categories ranging from advertising, branding and digital marketing to analytics, market research and data visualization. The entries were reviewed by a panel of 16 judges and evaluated based on the strategic approach, quality of work and ultimate success of each program or campaign.

A special thank you to our sponsors who are essential to the success of the AMYs - Friendly Human, Clear Channel Outdoor, The College Football Hall of Fame, Mobile App Hero, FSI Fulfillment, Zambezi, Pandora, Pivot, Nebo Agency, Headshot Ventures, & Uber.

Tonight’s event would not be possible without the tireless work of Dawn Hill and Brandi Houle, the AMYs co-chairs, and their team of volunteers, including: Veronica Kovachi, Sarah Carter, Nicole Ratteau, Louise Gordon, Theresa Schwanke, Orissa El, Jim Reynolds, Callie Holden, Kerry Abner, & Joey Schoenfeld.

Congratulations To The Finalists, And Enjoy The Evening!
