About AMA Atlanta

Setting the benchmark for marketing excellence

AMA Atlanta is the go-to-source for marketing knowledge, events, resources and networking opportunities in Atlanta. With almost 1,000 members, we are proud to be the largest marketing organization in Atlanta and the third largest chapter of the American marketing Association. As a result, members of AMA Atlanta have access to a wide variety of incredible resources both locally and internationally. AMA Atlanta is home for all marketers -- from high school students in the DECA Program and collegiate marketers to young professionals and experienced executives. Our membership represents all aspects of marketing from research, analytics and strategic planning to advertising, branding and communications. In addition to our dedicated Board of Directors, we are guided by the expertise and experience of our Executive Advisory Board consisting of past AMA Presidents and executive level marketers from some of Atlanta’s leading organizations.

Our Mission

AMA Atlanta is committed to Atlanta's marking community and strives to provide our members with the resources and networking opportunities that will enable them to grow both personally and professionally.

Our programs, resources and events are designed to help you: - grow your businesses - expand your network - enhance your knowledge - advance your career

You can count on AMA Atlanta to provide you with the tools, thought leadership and industry contacts that will help keep you on top of the latest marketing trends, industry best practices and developments in advanced technology.

Our passion for marketing comes through in everything we do from our sponsored events to our website content. We offer the most powerful program line-up and membership base in the city -- providing you with the opportunity to learn, connect and grow.

Please join us.

Annual Programming

The Signature Luncheon Series:

Each month, AMA Atlanta hosts a networking luncheon which is open to all members during which time a special guest speaker presents their expertise and insight on a key industry topic or trend. Each presentation is designed to be informative, enlightening and thought provoking.

New Member Orientation:

In conjunction with each luncheon, new AMA members are invited to a New Member Orientation where you can meet our Board of Directors while learning more about the organization along with the various volunteer opportunities available to you as a member.

Special Interest Groups (SIGs):

For members with a specific interest in digital marketing, social media, communications, analytics, or have a focus on nonprofit, multi-cultural or entertainment, we offer several Special Interest Groups (SIGs). Each SIG hosts a series of events that provide members with the opportunity to come together, network and explore topics specific to their areas of interest.

Networking Mixers:

These are fun events that provide you with the opportunity to meet other industry professionals in a more casual setting.
